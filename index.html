BEING WAVES

A New Way to Experience Music in VR

WHAT IS

Being Waves is an album-long virtual reality experience that challenges our ideas about reality and perception, giving life to a mesmerising audio responsive environment, set to run the entire duration of the upcoming release by Kinkajous. Our goal is to generate an innovative, engaging and collective musical experience for audiences to share.

ABOUT

Being Waves is a project by Kinkajous and Studio ANRK. Kinkajous is a London based band, led by drummer/producer Benoît Parmentier and saxophonist/ clarinettist Adrien Cau, exploring the space between jazz, electronic and orchestral music. Studio ANRK is an R&D studio exploring the future of spatial, immersive entertainment creating experiences built in virtual and augmented reality, and exploring the metaverse concept.
